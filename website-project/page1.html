Regions

There are seven regions known for the pokemon world that are in the main games. The seven regions being Kanto, Johto, Hoenn, Sinnoh, Unova, Kalos and Alola. In side games there five other regions named Orre, Fiore, Almia, Ransei and Oblivia. But the first seven are the main ones.

Kanto was the first region to be introduced in the games series. Like a lot of the regions there is a theme regarding the names of the towns and cities in Kanto. The theme for Kanto is colors. Some exaples are Pallet Town, Pewter City, Lavender Town and Cinnabar Island.

The second region to be introduced was Johto. Johto is located west of the Kanto region. The theme for this region is plants or something relating to a plant. Some examples of names of cities and towns are as follows, New Bark Town, Azalea Town, Cherrygrove City, Mahogany Town and Blackthorn City.

The next regiion to be introduced is Hoenn, which is located southwest of Kanto and Johto. The theme of this regions names is putting two words together. An exaple of some of the names are, Oldale Town, Littleroot town, Mossdeep City, Lilycove City and Mossdeep City.

Next up is the Sinnoh region which is north of the Kanto region. This particular region has no underlying theme in its names. Some town and city names are Celestic Town, Solaceon Town, Hearthome City, Snowpoint City and Sunyshore City.

The Unova region is the next to be introduced and is located so far away from the other regions that a boat is needed to get there. The theme of this regions names is weather, specifically clouds. Some names are Icirrus City, Nimbasa City, Driftveil City, Nuvema Town and Accumula Town.

The Kalos region is the sixth region to be introduced and is based off of France. Examples of some of the names are Vainville Town, Aquacorde Town, Lumiose City and Laverre City.

The Orre region which is based off of the Arizona desert is located on the northwest portion of the Kanto-Johto landmass. Examples of names in this region are Phenac City, Pyrite Town, The Under, Agate Village and Gateon Port.

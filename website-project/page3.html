Games

Another aspect of Pokemon other than the show is that there are a bunch of games spanning over the seven generations of Pokemon. ( All of the dates that I am mentioning below are the release dates of the games in Japan.)

In the first generation 4 games were released from 1996 to 1998 all released for the Game Boy. There was Pokemon Red, Green and Blue all of which were released in 1996. pokemon Yellow was released in 1998.

In the second generation only 2 games were released from 1999 to 2000 for the Game Boy Color. Pokemon Gold and Silver was released in 1999 and Pokemon Crystal was released in 2000.

Third generation had 3 games released spanning from 2002 to 2004. pokemon ruby and saphire was the first to be released of the new generation in 2002. The next two were both releasd in 2004, Pokemon FireRed and LeafGreen and Pokemon Emerald.

Fourth generation had 3 games released spanning from 2006 to 2009. Pokemon Diamond and Pearl was released in 2006. Pokemon Platinum is next to be released in 2008 and Pokemon HartGold and SoulSilver was released in 2009.

Fifth generation had 2 games released in 2010 and 2012. Pokemon Black and White and then Pokemon Balck 2 and White 2.

Sixth generation had 2 games released in 2013 and 2014. Pokemon X and Y and Pokemon Omega Ruby and Alpha Sapphire

Seventh generation had 2 gamesreleased in 2016 and 2017. Pokemon Sun and Moon and Pokemon Ultra Sun and Ultra Moon.

Gyms

Gyms are typically a place where trainers go to train their Pokemon, similar to a real gym where you would go to work out and build strength. A trainer can go to a gym to sharpentheri battling skills and where Pokemon can gain experience.

Most gyms specialize in a certain particular type of Pokemon, such as electric, fire, water or psychic. the most powerful trainer in any given gym is called the gym leader.

Most gyms are designed to follow and suit the type the gym specializes in. However, there are some gyms that do not practice this.

Official gyms are certified by a Pokemon league and atleast eight gym badges are required before entering a League Competition.
